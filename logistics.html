Logistics

FREIGHT FORWARDING (AIR, OCEAN & LAND) / CHARTERING / WAREHOUSING / EVENT LOGISTICS HANDLING / INDUSTRIAL PROJECTS HANDLING

Justifiably, our successful growth since being established in 1994, has been attributed to a well-defined goal, strategic planning, dynamic leadership and a team of self-motivated and well-experienced professionals, trained to give the best in ultimate customer satisfaction. The organizational structure is meticulously crafted with the division of labor principle, wherein a seasoned and proficient management team oversees the various departments with expertise and experience.

OUR SCOPE OF SERVICES

  • Air / Ocean clearing and forwarding
  • Events Cargo Handling
  • Tailored Freight Solutions for Sports Events
  • Warehousing & Distribution
  • Packing and Removals
  • Bonded Trucking & Special World-Wide Door-to-door Delivery
  • Exhibition and Industrial Project Shipment Handling
  • Air / Ocean Charter Services / Ground handling

A team of seasoned professionals, with extensive experience in the field, manages the aforementioned tasks. We maintain strong affiliations with Customs, AIR & SEA Port Authorities, Airlines and Shipping Agents, as well as Freight Forwarders, enabling us to seamlessly and efficiently facilitate the movement of cargo and passengers to the utmost satisfaction of our clients and agents.

We provide cost-effective and expeditious import/export freight and air charter services, serving nearly any country, facilitated by our dedicated commitment to regular operations.

Our success is largely attributed to continuous assessment and a 24-hour responsiveness to our customer's needs. Serving a diverse clientele ranging from small enterprises to national and high-profile multinational companies, we maintain confidence in the future and remain dedicated to delivering unparalleled value for money and top-notch service quality. We prioritize our customers as the cornerstone of our business, fostering a partnership approach wherein their success is intricately tied to our own.
