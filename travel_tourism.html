Ok to Board Messages

The 'Ok to Board' message is an essential requirement for passengers traveling from India and Pakistan with Tourist visas. To facilitate a smooth process, all 'Ok to Board' requests can be submitted 48 hours prior to your scheduled departure. For guests traveling on Tourist/Short-term visas, it is imperative to possess a valid onward and return ticket, accompanied by a clear copy of the visa. This mandatory procedure ensures compliance with travel regulations and streamlines the boarding process, providing passengers with a secure and hassle-free travel experience.
