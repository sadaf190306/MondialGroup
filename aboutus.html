OUR STORY

Established in 1994, Mondial Group has emerged as a dynamic conglomerate headquartered in Dubai, strategically located next to Dubai Terminal 1 Airport. With a rich history spanning nearly three decades, our company has successfully diversified its portfolio across key sectors, becoming a powerhouse of profitability and excellence.

OUR HISTORY

Founded in 1994, Mondial Group began as a logistics specialist near Dubai International Airport. Over the years, we expanded into travel (2006) and trading (2012), becoming a trusted name across industries. Our journey reflects Dubai's own transformation into a global hub.

OUR MISSION

To deliver seamless, innovative solutions that bridge global markets through exceptional logistics, travel experiences, and trading partnerships. We combine local expertise with international standards to create value for our clients.
