ABOUT US

Mondial Group, since its establishment in 1994 has transformed into a dynamic Dubai-based conglomerate. Over nearly three decades, we've excelled in Logistics, Travel, Overseas General Trading, and Education. From pioneering supply chain solutions in 1994 to expanding into Travel in 2006 and Overseas General Trading in 2012, our strategic evolution positions us as a powerhouse. Headquartered near Dubai International Terminal 1 Airport, we operate at the nexus of strategic operations and innovations, serving esteemed clientele such as Zee Tv, Ten Sports and IMG-Reliance across industries.
